Sri Lanka Cricket

The current President of Sri Lanka Cricket, Hon. Thilanga Sumathipala’s passion for the gentleman’s game started during his schoolboy days at Nalanda College, where he not only played for the XI Cricket team, but also assumed responsibilities as the Vice Captain and was awarded Cricket Colours. His talent and love for the game continued after his school days as he represented the Colombo Cricket Club – Division 1, First Class Cricket as well as the Blackheath Cricket Club in the UK.
